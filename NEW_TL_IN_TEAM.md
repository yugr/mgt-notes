Things that I find important for TL to agree upon
(with PM and other team members)
before starting with new team:
  - consensus decisions are ideal and
    everyone should try their best to
    achieve them (through technical discussions,
    building prototypes, etc.)
    but sometimes they are not possible
    (due to lack of time, presentation skills,
    competence, etc.); in such cases
    TL must make the final decision and other team
    members agree to follow (or leave)
  - TODOs and action items can (and should) be discussed
    (in good time before the deadline,
    not during the meeting where they should
    have been reported) but can NOT be ignored
  - to foster (or at least simplify) collaboration,
    all communication must be professional, respectful
    and polite
